Welcome to Polar Paths!

Our mission is to encourage members of the Bowdoin community to use public transportation whenever possible. This site is a hub where users can find information on Brunswick's public transport options, browse popular destinations, and coordinate carpools to events. Want to contribute to our project? Head to "Suggest" in the navigation bar or down below to recommend your own destinations.
