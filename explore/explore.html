Destinations

Portland State Theatre

The Portland State Theatre is one of the largest music venues in Maine and has several great shows. Take the bus or train!

Acadia National Park

Acadia National Park has some stunning natural beauty. Take the bus there!

South Portland Skatepark

This new skatepark is probably the only great park in Maine, you should go. Take the bus!

Lewiston, ME

Lewiston is another town worth visitng, just a bus ride away from Brunswick!

Bath, ME

Bath has a bunch of ships maybe worth looking at. Accessible by bus!

Ogunquit, ME

Ogunquit is in Southern Maine and is a cool beach town accessible by train!
